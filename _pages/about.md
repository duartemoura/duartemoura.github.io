Hi, I’m Duarte! I’m pursuing a Research Master’s Degree in Machine Learning with a focus on Healthcare at Carlos III University of Madrid, while working as a Solutions Architect at Amazon Web Services (AWS). I hold a degree in Electronics Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico in Lisbon, Portugal—my hometown.

Through my academic and professional experiences, I have developed a strong interest in the field of AI. At AWS, I’ve gained practical knowledge of its foundations and applications, helping customers adopt AI-powered solutions to meet their needs. In my master’s program, I focus on understanding and developing techniques that contribute to the core foundations of machine learning. I’m looking forward to becoming more involved in research and advancing this field further.
